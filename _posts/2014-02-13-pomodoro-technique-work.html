The Pomodoro Technique is very beneficial to my workplace productivity!

I'm tempted to be lazy, surf the internet, do the "easy" jobs, but this time and work management technique has been a very helpful way to tackle the important goals first!

Basic Synopsis of Pomodoro:

  1. 25 minutes of focused intense work on one task.
  2. 5 minutes of a break - not an email catchup break.  A walk and a coffee/tea refresh and even closing my eyes for five minutes.
  3. Repeat.

I learned of Pomodoro from John Sonmez, the author of the Simple Programmer several months ago.  http://simpleprogrammer.com/2013/11/04/want-quit-job/

Taking John's advice I read through the Pomodoro Technique Illustrated, found it to be extremely valuable and an easy read, very helpful for me in this season of life!
