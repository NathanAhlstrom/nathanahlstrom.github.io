Busy Times!!

What is with all the busyness?  I feel it too.  Why are we so busy!?!?

Recently I've started to think it has a lot to do with my faithful sidekick...

He is so useful, convenient, and johnny-on-the-spot! But he is also a major distraction and doesn't bounce over joyfully to see you like some other distractions.

[caption id="attachment_194" align="aligncenter" width="300"] http://www.publicdomainpictures.net/view-image.php?image=35696[/caption]

No, not this distraction, I'm talking about Mr. Smartphone.

The main things that distract me about Mr. Smartphone (and gadget-friends) are:

  1. The notification light blinking on my Android phone is irresistible.
  2. Having my phone in my pocket at all makes it a temptation to check frequently.
  3. The red notification circle with a count on my iPad terrorizes me to read email, etc.

What should we do about this Mr. Smartphone?

Recently I've heard some ideas about reclaiming some of the smartphone distraction time. Several of these have helped me:

  1. Disable notifications on every application on my phone, both the blinking light and the status bar.
  2. Leave my phone plugged in AND away from its usual sidekick location.  After work, I leave my phone in the kitchen and NOT in my pocket; to spend that time undistracted.
  3. Turn off the phone entirely after work.  (I haven't done this yet)
  4. Schedule in a check my email segment of time after dinner or before bedtime.  Have the conversation with your family and make it okay to have five minutes at a designated time to check in.  Knowing you have this time later will help you stay in the moment.
  5. On my iPad, I haven't found a way to turn off those notification badges...they still terrorize me.  My solution is to not use the iPad.  It sits in the drawer!

What about you?  How do you get undistracted time with your family and friends?  How do you keep your smartphone from distracting you?
